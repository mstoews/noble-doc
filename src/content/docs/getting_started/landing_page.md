## Simplify Condo Management and Stay Audit-Ready

Noble Ledger is purpose-built accounting and management software for condominium corporations, property managers, and nonprofits. Replace manual processes with secure, fast, and compliant financial workflows — from day-to-day bookkeeping to audit preparation.

---

## What's Inside

### Getting Started

New to Noble Ledger? The [Getting Started guide](/getting_started/getting_started) walks you through initial setup, including your Chart of Accounts (COA), financial statement mapping, and account allocation for condominiums.

### Features

- **[Unit Owner View](/noble_ledger/unit_owner_view)** — Owners can access condo fees, maintenance requests, voting, documents, and contact information from a single dashboard.
- **[Condo Board Policies](/noble_ledger/condo_board_policies)** — Role-based access, approval workflows, data integrity controls, and lightning-fast reporting for boards and managers.
- **[Payment Modernization](/blog/traditional_payments)** — Move beyond dual-signature cheques. Noble Ledger integrates with Plaid to support secure Electronic Funds Transfers (EFTs), reducing fraud risk and operational overhead.

Learn the fundamentals that power Noble Ledger's design:

- [History of Accounting](/accounting/history_of_accounting) — From tally sticks to double-entry bookkeeping.
- [Accounting vs. Bookkeeping](/accounting/account_vs_bookkeeping) — Roles, responsibilities, and qualifications explained.
- [Cash vs. Accrual Accounting](/accounting/cash_vs_accrual) — Understanding financial performance and liquidity for boards.

- [What is a Condominium Corporation](/accounting/what_is_condo_corp) — Legal structure and obligations under Saskatchewan's Condominium Property Act, 1993.

### Compliance and Audit Requirements

- **[Condo Assurance Requirements](/guides/condo_requirements)** — Saskatchewan audit and review thresholds based on unit count (fewer than 12, 12–50, or more than 50 units).
- **[Security Policy](/policy/security)** — PCI Level 1 certification, SOC 1 & SOC 2 compliance, and EMVCo standards.
- **[Privacy Policy](/policy/private)** — How personal information is collected, used, and protected.
- **[Roles & Teams](/policy/roles)** — Invite team members, assign roles, and track activity logs for up to 180 days.
